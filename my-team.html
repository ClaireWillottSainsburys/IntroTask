Software Development Team Roles and Responsibilities

My Role - Associate Engineer

As an Associate Engineer at the start of my career, I am focused on learning new skills, the technology environment of Sainsbury's and how I am going to fit it. My responsibilities include:

  • Supporting software development by writing and testing code
  • Troubleshooting technical issues and debugging errors
  • Learning from by reviewing code, shadowing senior and more experience staff

Senior Software Engineer - Phil

Phil, a Senior Software Engineer, plays a leadership role in Team Eve. Phil's key responsibilities include:

  • Leading software design and architecture discussions with full knowledge of the Sainsbury's Engineering Guide and standards
  • Leading Scrum artifacts such as backlog refinement and sprint reviews
  • Reviewing code written by other team members
  • Writing and reviewing complex code to ensure efficiency and maintainability
  • Providing mentorship and guidance to new engineers like myself

Software Tester - Jeevi

Jeevi is a Software Tester responsible for ensuring our applications are high-quality and run as per the project design. Key tasks include:

  • Writing and executing Automatic Test Suite (ATS) repositories that test new projects for functional, performance, and security.
  • Ensuring the ATS repositories are onboarded to our CI-CD framework
  • Collaborating with engineers to identify and fix bugs early in development
  • Validating that the software meets business requirements before release

Business Analyst - Dan

Dan, as a Business Analyst (BA) and his position bridges the gap between business needs and technical implementation. His responsibilities include:

  • Gathering and analyzing project requirements from stakeholders
  • Translating business goals into clear documentation which has company-wide visibility
  • Supporting product owners and engineers in refining project scope
  • Acting as a liason netween Team Eve and other technical/non-technical teams

Engineers - Saif, Andi, and Ajay

Saif, Andi, and Ajay are engineers who work alongside me in the development process. Their responsibilities include:

  • Designing and implementing new projects and features
  • Debugging and resolving issues found in testing
  • Collaborating with testers and business analysts to ensure smooth project execution
  • Improving system performance and scalability through best practices

Reflections & Takeaways

Having a clear undserstanding of where everyone's roles sit within Team Eve and the larger Sainbury's Tech team as a whole has helped me see the bigger picture of the mosaic that is Sainbury's tech. Each team is different but when you step back and look at the whole picture everything fits together.
